Abstract

Deepfakes are evolving rapidly with GANs, diffusion models, and neural rendering systems. Their misuse now impacts society, crime, politics, and cybersecurity. This website provides an extended, visually-enhanced, real-time insight into detection techniques, forensic methods, and real case studies.

Real-Time Deepfake Examples

CEO Voice Fraud (2025 – Bengaluru)

Cybercriminals cloned a CEO's voice and tricked the finance team into transferring ₹1.2 crore. AI reproduced breathing pauses and emotional tone perfectly.

Political Manipulation (2024–25)

Deepfake videos of Indian politicians went viral during election season, shaping public opinion using fabricated speeches.

Fake Interviews (2026)

Candidates used real-time face filters to impersonate skilled professionals in video interviews.
